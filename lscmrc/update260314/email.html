Nominate two varieties (mice) for the Jubilee Cup 2026

Show Secretary, Terry Sales, is wanting your nominations for the Jubilee Cup for 2026. At the beginning of the year you nominate two varieties of your choice, and you will gain points for wins at members' shows with those varieties. The Jubilee Cup was introduced in 1965 and was a very popular competition in its early days with 11 exhibitors nominating varieties in one year. You cannot normally be retrospectively given points for the two varieties. We have made an exception this year. Even though the competition began in February we will accept your variety nominations provided they are received prior to the April Show, perhaps when making your entries. The results during the year for the Jubilee Cup have not appeared as it was necessary for it to be manually calculated. This has changed and they are now computer calculated as with all other points.

The 2025 results for the Jubilee Cup are as follows:

A Blue Tan BIS sensation in 1964 - An LSCMRC podcast asks, Is this win still relevant today?

Has the Blue Tan ever had a fair deal? Suddenly, over 60 years ago, in 1964, an u8 Blue Tan won BIS at a Calder Valley Mouse Club Show. Was it controversial? Yes. And a new Podcast looks at the heated debates of the time and asks Is this win relevant today? Or should we let the arguments rot in the dustbin of forgotten history? We say that, sadly, the current mouse fancy never gets the exposure to this type of fascinating debate. Somehow it just doesn't work on social media which is more geared to barbed sound-bites, rather than carefully reasoned and intelligent argument among fellow fanciers. So LSCMRC, in another innovation, is experimenting with podcasts to bring history and its protagonists back to life with AI voice creation. We welcome helpers from our members, including script-writers and producers and sound recordists. You don't need to be professional, just keen to be involved. If you judge then expect to be asked at the end of the show for your overall comments. And don't worry, we will edit out all umm's and err's and glitches so you will sound like a pro!
